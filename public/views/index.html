Snake & Ladder
Game

Snake and Ladder is a classic board game played by two players. The game involves a numbered board with squares, each representing a position. Players take turns rolling a die and advancing their game pieces accordingly. The presence of ladders allows players to climb to higher positions swiftly, while snakes force them to descend.
